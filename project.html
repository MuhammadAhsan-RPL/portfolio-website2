One School E-Report

The official digital reporting platform for reporting student issues directly to teachers and guardians.

Problem

Maintaining confidentiality of sensitive reports while ensuring real-time status transparency was a major challenge.

Solution

Built a closed-loop system with dual validation (teacher + admin) and live status tracking: Incoming → Actioned → Completed.

Impact

Reduced response time from 3 days to under 2 hours. Used by 500+ students across 3 schools with zero data leaks.

Role & Technology

Role: Full-stack developer & UI/UX designer
Tech: HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, Firebase
